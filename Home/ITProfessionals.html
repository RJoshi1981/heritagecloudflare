ITProfessionals

Heritage InfoTech Technologies, Heritage InfoTech Staffing Group’s technology specialty division, is a national IT staffing organization, operating across industries, that provides contract, direct (permanent), consulting and project management services to top companies and their IT departments.

From staff augmentation and project/business specific solutions to direct (permanent) placement services, Heritage InfoTech Technologies’ dedicated team of recruiting and staffing experts consistently delivers quality IT professionals, including those with active security clearances, covering all the latest technologies, on a time-sensitive basis.

Heritage InfoTech Technologies places IT contractors in all 50 states by seamlessly coordinating recruiting resources in local and regional markets with Heritage InfoTech Technologies National Recruiting & Delivery, a dedicated national recruiting team that focuses exclusively on national accounts, supporting regional recruiting operations and delivering contractors to markets where Heritage InfoTech does not have physical office locations.

Whether start-ups or the Fortune 500, the public or private sector, Heritage InfoTech Technologies matches world class talent with exceptional opportunities keeping all involved way ahead of the curve in the dynamic IT marketplace.
